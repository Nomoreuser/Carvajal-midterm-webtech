Sypnosis

Jujutsu Kaisen 0 is a feature-length prequel to Jujutsu Kaisen, based on Gege Akutami’s manga Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical School. The film follows Yuta Okkotsu, a high school student burdened by the curse of his childhood friend, Rika Orimoto, who perished in a tragic accident. Rika’s spirit manifests as an immensely powerful and uncontrollable curse, attracting the attention of both dangerous entities and elite sorcerers. Recognizing Yuta's potential, renowned sorcerer Satoru Gojo enrolls him at Tokyo Jujutsu High, where he trains alongside Maki Zenin, Toge Inumaki, and Panda to harness his abilities. As Yuta grows in strength, he becomes entangled in a larger conflict with Suguru Geto, a former jujutsu sorcerer who seeks to eradicate non-sorcerers and reshape the world according to his ideals.
